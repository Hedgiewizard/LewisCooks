Fun Snack Ideas

  Sometimes breakfast, lunch and dinner just isn't enough. Sometimes you need some extra food in the middle of the day. Most of these will be pretty simple but on some there will be a link to help you. Here are some snaks that we frequently eat.

  Apples- Apples can be eaten with cheese, penutbutter, or almondbutter.

  Banana- Banana can be eaten with cheese, penutbutter, or almondbutter.

  Trail Mix- People will eat this on hikes but they could realy be eaten at all times. They are realy good.... or just a hunt for the M&Ms.

  Apricots- Dried or fresh, delicious apricots are low in saturated fat, cholesterol and sodium, are a fantastic source of fiber, potassium, and Vitamins A and C.

  Bell Pepper- Bell Peppers are a great snack. just slice some up and eat them, they are simple and refreshing.

  Plums- From appetizers to desserts, fresh or cooked, beautiful, delectable plums are a treat for both the eyes and the tastebuds.

  Mangos- Mangos are said to be some of the most widely consumed fruit in the world. One problem they are hard to cut.
