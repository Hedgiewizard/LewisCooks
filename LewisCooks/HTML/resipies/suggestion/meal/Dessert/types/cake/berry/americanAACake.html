Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 1.25-1.5 hours

What you need... A scale and this...

     250 grams of self-raising flour
     1 teaspoon(s) baking powder
     225 grams of caster sugar
     2 eggs
     0.5 teaspoon(s) of almond extract
     150 grams of butter melted
     100 grams of ready-to-eat dried apricots snipped into pieces
     25 grams of flaked almonds

How To Do It

    1.   Lightly grease the cake tin and line the bace with a circle of non-stick baking parchment. Pre-heat the oven to 325F. Put the flour, baking powder, sugar, eggs, almond extract and melted butter into a large bowl. Mix well to combine, then beat well for 1 minute; and electric mixer is best for this but of course you can also beat by hand with a wooden spoon. Add the apples and apricots to the bowl and gently mix them in.
    2.   spoon the mixture into the prepared tin, gently level the suface and sprinkle with the flaked almonds.
    3.   Bake for 1-1.5 hours, until the cake is golden, firm to the touch and beginning to shrin away from the sides of the tin. Allow to cool in the tin for a few minutes, then turn out on to a plate to serve.
