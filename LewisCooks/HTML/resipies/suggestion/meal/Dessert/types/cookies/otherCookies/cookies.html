Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 12-15 minutes
Makes 40 small cookies

What you need for the Cookie Dough

     1 Cups Butter (room temperature)
     1.5 Cups Brown Sugar(we like to make homemade here is a link on how to)
     0.5 Cup(s) White Sugar
     2 Eggs
     1 Tbsp Vanilla Extract
     3 Cups White Flour
     1 tsp baking soda
     0.5 tsp salt
     2 Cups Milk Chocolate Chips(We use Ghirardelli)
     (optional)0.75 Cup(s) Toasted Pecan pieces

How To Do It

    1.  Preheat the oven to 350 degrees farenhight.
    2.  In a stand mixer cream butter.
    3.  Once creamed, add the brown sugar and the white sugar. Mix until butter and sugars are creamed together and well combined.
    4.  Then add one egg at a time; mix in, then add the other egg. Add the vanilla extract and mix on medium speed until all ingredients are well combined.
    5.  In a separate bowl sift 3 cups of white flour(you can sift if you'd like). Add baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Mix all dry ingredients together.
    6.  Add dry ingredients into the mixing bowl with the butter/sugar mix. Add the dry mix slowly while the mixer is on low. Continue to mix for about 2 minutes. Once combined, add the chocolate chips a cup at a time. If you want to add toasted pecans do it at this stage.
    7.  Once dough is combined then scoop out approximately 1 to 1 ½ Tbsp of dough onto a cookie sheet. Make the dough into a loose ball with about an inch separating each ball. Don’t overly compact the dough in the ball or the cookie won’t spread well. Place in the oven and cook at 350 for 12 minutes.
    8.  When you first remove them they should seem just a little undone. They should look like they could use 2ish more minutes. That. That is when you take them out. Remove from oven and transfer to a wire rack to cool for 10 minutes.
