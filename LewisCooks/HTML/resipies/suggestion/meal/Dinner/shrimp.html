Grilled Honey Sriracha Lime Shrimp

How many servings do you want?(write it in decimal format)

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes

What you will need

      3 Tbsp Butter
      2 Cloves of garlic minced
      1 tsp of smoked paprika
      3 Tbsp Sriracha
      4 Tbsp Honey
      1 Tbsp Lime Juice
      24 Shrimp
      6 Bamboo Skewers

DIRECTIONS

    1.   Heat grill to 500 degrees.
    2.   While grill is heating up prepare the sauce. In a saucepan add butter over medium heat. Once melted add garlic and let cook for about a minute. Add paprika, sriracha, honey, and lime juice. Stir all ingredients and simmer for about 4-5 minutes. Remove from heat.
    3.   Place 6 shrimp on each skewer. Place on hot grill and cook on each side for 2-3 minutes. After turning the first time baste shrimp with honey sriracha sauce. Flip after 2-3 minutes and baste again. Flip again after another minute and baste both sides one more time. Once shrimp are completely cooked remove from grill.
