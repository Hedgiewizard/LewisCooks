Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes

      1 olive oil
      4oz pecans
      8oz brussel sprouts
      0.5 tsp lemon juice

DIRECTIONS

    1.  Bring olive oil to medium heat in a stovetop and oven safe dish. Add 4oz pecans to the pan and coat with the olive oil, then add a pinch of sea salt. Toast lightly by stirring pecans in pan every 1-2 minutes. Remove from pan once pecans start to brown. Dish will be reused. Do not discard or clean because you will want the pecan infused oil to cook your brussels sprouts in.
    2.  Remove stem and half 8 oz of brussels sprouts. Place halves cut side down in the previously used, seasoned and oiled oven safe dish. Use Bake at 400* for 15 minutes or top is starting to brown. Remove from oven. Combine toasted pecans and brussels sprouts into a bowl. Toss with Â½ tsp lemon juice. Salt pepper to taste.
