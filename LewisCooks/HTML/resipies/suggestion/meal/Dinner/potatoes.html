Roasted Red Potatoes with Feta

  These tasty potatoes are sure to fill up your family and become a new family favorite. Click to skip

How many servings do you want?(write it in decimal format)

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes

What you will need

      3lb-5lb 0.5" thick cut red potatoes (enough to fill 9x13 pan per serving)
      2-3 cloves of Garlic
      3 Tbsp Butter
      salt and peper(to taste)
      1 tsp dried Sage
      1 tbsp dried Rosemary
      8 oz Feta (6 oz for mixing with potato and mushrooms on the stove and the remaining 2 oz for topping when you return to the oven)
      6 oz Mushrooms

DIRECTIONS

    1.   Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F.
    2.   Roast potato, half the garlic, and 2Tbsp butter at 450 for 8-10 minutes (or until butter is melted) in a 16x9 pan. Once butter is melted, stir the potatoes, garlic and butter to coat the potatoes. Roast for about 25-30 min more (until potatoes are al dente). Add sage, rosemary, salt, and pepper. Roast for 20 - 25 min until the potatoes are golden with a slight crust on the outside and a soft.
    3.   Ten minutes before potatoes are done in oven, melt remaining Tbsp of butter in a large pan on medium heat. Add chopped garlic to the butter and sauté for a minute. Add cut mushrooms to the pan and sauté. The mushrooms will reduce in size so feel free to add the whole package if you like because they will reduce.
    4.   Once potatoes are soft, transfer cooked potatoes to pan with the mushrooms and garlic and stir together. Add 6 oz of feta cheese on medium heat. The butter and cheese should melt together (enough to be creamy but not totally covered so use less feta if 6 -7 oz looks like too much). Add a little more butter depending on how creamy you would like the cheese/butter mixture.
    5.   Return the combined ingredients to the 9x13 pan. Add remaining feta to the top of the potatoes. At this point you can bake or broil it all until slightly crispy on top, about 5-10 min.
