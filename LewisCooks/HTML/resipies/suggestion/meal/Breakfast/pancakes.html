Pancakes

  You're in a good mood and you want a classic breakfast. Pancakes are a great choice. They are easy and tasty. We make these all the time. They are great with side of eggs and bacon. Click to skip

Ok, now that's out of the way lets get started.

How many servings do you want?(write it in decimal format)

What you will need

      1 cup all-purpose flour
      1 tablespoon sugar
      2 teaspoon baking powder
      0.25 teaspoon salt
      1 beaten egg
      1 cup milk
      2 tablespoons cooking oil

DIRECTIONS

     In a medium bowl stir together the flour(1 cup), sugar(1 tablespoon), baking powder(2 teaspoons), and salt(¼ teaspoon). Make a well in the center if the dry mixture; set aside.
     In a another medium bowl or in a liquid measuring cup holds at at least 2 cups(it makes less dishes to mix them in the measuring cup just make sure you add the milk first) combine the milk(1 cup milk), egg(1), and cooking oil(2 tablespoons).
     Add the liquid mixture to the dry mixture and than stir until combined.(this mixture should be lumpy)
     For standard-size pancakes, pour ¼ cup batter on to a hot, lightly greased griddle or heavy skillie. Cook over medium heat for about 2 minutes or until pancakes are golden brown, turning to second side when pancakes have bubble surfaces and edges are slightly dry. Serve warm. Top with syrup, whipped cream, powdered sugar, and/or fruit.
