Breakfast Burrito

  You woke up wanting a burrito but it is still early. Is it too early? Mabye for a meat burrito but what about a breakfast burrito. They are fast and easy to make. Click to skip

Ok, now that's out of the way lets get started.

What you will need

      Tortilla
      Eggs
      ham/meats(optional)
      Cheese(optional)
      Hash browns(optional)
      Bell pepper(optional)
      Avocado(optional)
      Salsa(optional)
      Bacon(optional)

DIRECTIONS

    1.  Make a tortilla, my family uses TortillaLand tortillas(if there is a Walmart near you you can get them there) To heat up these tortillas you place them on a warm pan until it creates bubbles and the bubble’s become golden where they are touching the pan. Than set aside.
    2.  Than make eggs. Two should be plenty if you aren’t putting to much into it. If you are planning on a stuffed burrito than only make one egg.
    3.  Put The eggs and anything else you want in your burrito on the tortilla and roll it up.(if you want to put taco meat or something in there go ahead)
