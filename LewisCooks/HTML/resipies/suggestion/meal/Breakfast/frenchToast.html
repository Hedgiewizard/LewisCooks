French Toast

How many servings do you want?(write it in decimal format)

What you will need

      Bread
      4 Eggs
      0.5 teaspoon Cinnamon
      0.5 teaspoon Vanilla
      0.125 cup milk or just a splash

DIRECTIONS

    1.  In a flat shallow container mix together eggs, cinnamon, vanilla.
    2.  Than quickly supmerge each side of each slice of bread.
    3.  After you do that place it on a warm pan.
    4.  Once both sides are barely golden take off heat. Eat warm. Top with syrup, whipped cream, powdered sugar, and/or fruit.
