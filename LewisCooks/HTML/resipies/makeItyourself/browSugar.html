Brown Sugar

  Run out of brown sugar or you want to try something new? Make your own! It is fast and, easy.

How many servings do you want?(write it in decimal format)

2 C White sugar 2.5 Tbsp Molasses

  Combine the white sugar and molasses(add little extra for a richer taste) together in a bowl or even in your electric mixer with the wire whisks. Once combined set aside.
